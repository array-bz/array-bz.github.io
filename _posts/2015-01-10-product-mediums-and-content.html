--- layout: post title: "Product Mediums and Content" date: 2015-01-10 ---

This is a purist's issue with the experience of digital content.

Technology products glorify the medium at the expense of intimacy with the content.
Spotify, Windows, YouTube, Facebook, iTunes, even Pages.
The user wants to experience the content.
The company spends a lot of time on the medium.
The significance of the medium get's sold to the user.
The user is interested in the medium only by proxy of the content.
In the purest experience, the medium would be completely invisible and the user would interact only with the content.
Branding competes for attention with experience of content.
