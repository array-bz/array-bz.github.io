To the next programmer,

    I aim to write the highest quality code possible.
    I want to be able to look you in the eye and say I have
    written maintainable, extensible code.
    If your code review surfaces short comings,
    I'd like to hear about it.
    There may even be a beer in it for you.


To the Product Owner,

    Technology can be a minefield. I employ processes and
    practices which aim to give you as much control as possible.

    This means communicating the total cost of development and
    providing a thorough account as possible of your options
    and the benefits and drawbacks of each.
